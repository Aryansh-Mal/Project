MINDINSTALL team helps the user to manipulate the data.

This application is made for the UI representation of the KPN products with their description and images. So It can help the users to gain more knowledge about products familywise. It can also help in providing the KT regarding product families, categories, Product Catalogue through User Interface.

Through this application specific Users with proper authorization can perform the CRUD operations on product through UI which directly reflects on Product Catalogue. Also they can perform bulk update of the Product Catalogue.

Authenticated users can also see the graphical representation of the total price of the products and price of the sold products catagory wise and debtor wise which helps them to do the data analysis.
